Athena Inv V2.2 — Version Summary
=================================

Core purpose
------------
Multi-timeframe POI (Points of Interest) tracker and entry logic built on 4H structure, 1H FVGs, and continuation zones. Trades only with trend: BUY in bullish 4H, SELL in bearish 4H.

1. 4H structure & trend
-----------------------
• Swing points: 4H pivots (configurable swing length).
• Trend confirmed only when BOTH ChoCH and BoS occur:
  - ChoCH = close beyond previous swing.
  - BoS = close beyond last swing.
• Bias: 1 = bullish, -1 = bearish. No counter-trend entries.

2. Multi-POI tracking (v2.2)
----------------------------
• Supply/demand stored in arrays (not a single zone).
• POI types:
  - 4H: main swing zone, candle-before-impulse OB, 4H Breaker Block, optional 4H FVG.
  - 1H: ChoCH OB, continuation OB/FVG, 1H Breaker Block.
• Mitigation: tracks whether price has revisited each zone (unmitigated POIs highlighted).
• Capped at max_pois per bias; FIFO when full.

3. Breaker blocks
-----------------
• 4H Breaker = last opposite candle before ChoCH/BoS on 4H.
• 1H Breaker = same on 1H. Stored as POIs and drawn with distinct style (e.g. blue/teal).

4. 1H FVG & iFVG entry
----------------------
• FVG: 1H fair value gaps (body or full range), min size vs 1H ATR.
• Historical FVGs: stores recent 1H FVGs as price heads to 4H POI (up to max_hist_fvg).
• Inversion (iFVG): full 1H close through any stored gap:
  - Bullish FVG inverts (close below gap) → buy setup; entry = mid of iFVG.
  - Bearish FVG inverts (close above gap) → sell setup; entry = mid of iFVG.
• Valid when: price has revisited a 4H POI, or (in continuation window) touched a 1H POI.

5. Continuation POIs
--------------------
• After 4H structure change, new 1H OB/FVG zones from the move become POIs.
• Continuation window: for N bars after trend confirm, entries allowed at 1H continuation POIs in trend direction.
• Optional: 4H FVG as POI so revisits trigger 1H iFVG entry logic.

6. Entry models
---------------
• 4H POI + 1H ChoCH: price at 4H POI and 1H ChoCH → limit at 1H OB (candle before rally).
• 1H iFVG: HTF POI revisited + 1H FVG inverts → limit at mid of iFVG; multi-TP (TP1–TP4).
• 1H POI + liquidity: unmitigated 1H POI below EQL (buys) or above EQH (sells) → limit at POI (inducement).

7. Risk & display
-----------------
• SL: beyond recent 1H internal swing (e.g. swing high for sells) by ATR buffer.
• TPs: TP1–TP3 at fixed R:R; TP4 at previous 4H swing (liquidity).
• Premium/Discount: optional filter — sells above 50% equilibrium, buys below.
• Drawing: swings, BoS labels, POI boxes (unmitigated/Breaker styling), FVG boxes, iFVG zone, entry/SL/TP lines, limit levels; lines limited in length and hidden after N bars.

8. Alerts
---------
• 1H iFVG Sell / Buy.
• Any 1H iFVG entry.
• 4H POI + 1H ChoCH limit buy/sell.
• 1H POI + liquidity buy/sell.

In short
--------
This version unifies 4H structure, multi-POI tracking, Breaker Blocks, 4H FVG POIs, continuation logic, and three entry types (4H POI+ChoCH, 1H iFVG, 1H POI+liquidity) in one indicator with clear zones, entry levels, and alerts, while keeping trades aligned with 4H trend and optional premium/discount.
