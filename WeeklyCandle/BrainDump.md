So the idea behind this is that if price create a strong disrespect candle. it will often retrace to some pois inside it the target the Previous Candle low/ High depending on the nature of the candle. 

Bullish candle - target prev candle high. 
Bearish candle target prev candle low. 

indecision weekly candles. 
For indecision candles it depends on which level is price at. 

has price just taken out a recent strong liquidity i.e sweept a Swing low. in this case price might likely want to becomes bulish. 

if price has already reacted to a sweep or Weekly POI then the indecision candle can imply that price is continuing with the direction its already been taking. 

In that canse just enter at the current price of the indicision candle bse price might not retrace beyond that. the indicision its self is signifying a retracement. 


However other indicision candles especially at extremes do signify potential reversal moves. So need to plan for then accordingly. 

NB. Weeks that end in a rejection that is relatively equal to the size of the body usually the rejection wick is simply a pullback so these can offer quick entry if there is a supporing POI close by, 4H or Daily. 

Trading Style2. 
Weekly Reversals. 
1. Mark out weekly demand zones and supply zones. 
2. when price taps a weekly zone look out for reversal signs on the 4H timeframe. 

POI summary 
1. FVG
2. Orderblock (1) i.e supply and demand zones 
3. Candle before a sizable FVG (OB2)
4. Breaker Block
5. Swing points sweep

Note: The Order Block detector at times is not able to detect other POIS like Candle followed by an FVG. These areas need to also be marked out.

Entry
i.e 4H IFVG formation - entry when price taps into 15% the IFVG again. 

NB. such moves can be valid for like a full month. so be ready to hold them for this long. 

We also need to Tag them so that we know which kind of move are we trading.
