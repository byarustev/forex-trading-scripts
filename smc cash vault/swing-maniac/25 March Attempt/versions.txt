v1.5

1. Strong-zone counter-trend gate
Touch the Lux strong band (same band as your Strong box) → turn on a counter-trend leg mode (long from strong demand, short from strong supply).
While that mode is on: only trades in the direction of that leg; opposing strong/flow/fallback/pattern signals are off.
Exit when price hits the weak band, chart TF swing flips (tfSwingTrendBias vs armed value), or optional opposing External CHoCH on 4H.
Flow can arm with bias OR the gate; mixed External CHoCH blocks are relaxed when the gate matches.
Alerts / debug include a CT Gate state (CT_LONG / CT_SHORT / OFF).

2. IFVG fallback: extremes only for formation, not for killing the setup
Formation (flow touch + first FVG in the window) stays tied to the bottom/top fraction of the swing (ifvgFallbackSwingFrac, default 30%).
Violation, retest, and entry are not cancelled just because price goes to EQ and back — we removed the abort logic that cleared waits when price left the extreme band.
Violations and fallback entries no longer require the extreme band on every bar; tracking of newer FVGs after a candidate exists can happen without the extreme filter.
Split bearFvgFormedExtremeCtx / bullFvgFormedExtremeCtx for initial formation vs plain bearFvgFormedSel for tracking.

3. Chart leg “CHoCH + BOS” tracking + mixed-gate bypass
Tracks chart micro CHoCH (bullChoch15 / bearChoch15) and Lux swing BOS (tfBullBreak / tfBearBreak per side).
ctfLongLegFull / ctfShortLegFull = that side has seen both CHoCH and BOS before the opposing swing BOS clears it.
New input: Chart leg: bypass mixed gate if opposite leg incomplete — when your long leg is full and the short leg isn’t (or the reverse), external mixed CHoCH can still be bypassed for strong/flow so you can keep trading the initial side until the other side fully flips (both CHoCH + BOS), not on a “half” flip.
