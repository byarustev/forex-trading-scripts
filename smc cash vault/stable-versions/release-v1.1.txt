OVERVIEW
--------
Overlay indicator for Smart Money Concept (SMC) style setups: HTF bias, POI
proximity, and structured entry criteria with calculated entry, stop loss,
and take-profit levels. Designed around a configurable Bias TF (default 4H)
and Entry Context TF (default 1H), with optional faster 5m / 15m confirmation.


KEY FEATURES (THIS VERSION)
---------------------------
• HTF bias & structure
  - Bias timeframe (default 4H) drives directional bias (BULL / BEAR) from
    swing structure.
  - External pivot length and sweep lookback for confirmation logic.

• POI (Points of Interest)
  - HTF Order Block + Fair Value Gap packing for zone bounds.
  - Optional merge of LuxAlgo-style HTF swing Order Blocks into the POI box.
  - POI tap / proximity checks before signals.

• Entry criteria (priority: C1 → C2 → C3 → C4; fast mode: 5m > 15m > entry TF)
  - C1: CHOCH + OB on the confirm timeframe.
  - C2: Sweep + MSS on the confirm timeframe.
  - C3: Inverted FVG (iFVG) near POI; on entry TF, Athena-style multi-FVG
    history + inversion rules (configurable).
  - C4: Limit-style OB+FVG cluster inside HTF POI with approach filter.

• Risk & targets
  - SL modes: (1) Structure — beyond last HTF swing low/high (Bias TF) with
    pad, or (2) HTF POI box — beyond poiBot / poiTop with ATR pad; stepped
    fixes if SL is on the wrong side of entry.
  - TPs: TP1 = 1R; TP2 = HTF liquidity clamp between 2R–3R where applicable;
    TP3 = 5R.

• Chart & alerts
  - Entry line + optional SL / TP1–TP3 segments, labels, vertical connector.
  - Optional show/hide SL & TPs; entry label tooltip can carry full SL/TP text.
  - JSON alert payload: instruction, entryMode, criteria, bias, entry, SL,
    TP1–TP3, risk, asset / tickerId, confirm TF, slPlacement, timestamp.


WHAT IT DOES WELL (CURRENT FEEDBACK)
------------------------------------
• Strong at timing swing-style entries when aligned with the HTF (e.g. 4H)
  map — clean narrative from bias → POI → confirm → levels.
• Clear, rule-based SL and TP1–TP3 for each valid signal (subject to inputs).


KNOWN LIMITATIONS & FOLLOW-UPS
------------------------------
• Continuation areas: the indicator can still produce low-quality or “wrong”
  signals in continuation / trend-stretch contexts. Filtering or context rules
  for continuation vs reversal are a planned improvement.

• Internal / nuanced entries: some setups that are “inside” structure or
  zones can get SL distances that don’t match discretionary placement; SL
  logic refinement for those cases is on the backlog.


DISCLAIMER
