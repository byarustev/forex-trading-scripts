HTF demand / supply POI (LuxAlgo smart-money-concepts storeOrdeBlock logic)
- Zone is the extreme parsed candle on the slice from swing pivot bar through structure-break bar (not a fixed lookback from the break).
- Inputs: wide-bar wick swap optional (default 0 = raw OHLC, better on FX); break tolerance in × HTF ATR; fallback lookback and max slice capped for Pine.

FX-oriented defaults
- POI→liquidity filter off by default; lower min distance when enabled; iFVG proximity default slightly wider.

HTF support / resistance POI (LuxAlgo support-resistance-signals MTF)
- Pivot-based bands: resistance above pivot high, support below pivot low; thickness from (range/highest)×0.17×margin.
- Long bias unions latest HTF support band into POI; short bias unions latest HTF resistance band.

Debug
- Optional label includes merged HTF S&R band when enabled.
